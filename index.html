Why sadness is important to us?

One of the things that people always misunderstand is Sadness only have bad aspects. People always say that Sadness is one of the things that should be eliminated from this world. This section will explain why sadness is important.

Why we always want more happiness and how to stop it?

Love is beautiful, magical, inspirational, pleasure and many more. But do love only have happiness? In this section, we will talk about both side of love. Both happiness and sadness.
